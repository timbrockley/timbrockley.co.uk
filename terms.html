Photo Shoot Terms

TFP Shoot

a) The basis for this agreement is a non-commercial arrangement where the model, in exchange for modelling time, agrees that they shall receive photographs delivered, by the photographer, as digital images.

b) Copyright and all intellectual property rights to the photographs taken at the photo shoot are owned by the photographer.

c) Both the model and the photographer agree to use the photographs for personal promotion only and will not sell or commercialise any of the photographs unless agreed by both parties.

d) Third parties are not granted any rights to publish or distribute any of the photographs without the agreement of the model and the photographer.

TFP+ Shoot

a) The basis for this agreement is a non-commercial arrangement where the model, in exchange for modelling time, agrees that they shall receive photographs delivered, by the photographer, as digital images.

b) Copyright and all intellectual property rights to the photographs taken at the photo shoot are owned by the photographer.

c) Both the model and the photographer agree to use the photographs for personal promotion only and will not sell or commercialise any of the photographs unless agreed by both parties with the exception of section d.

d) The model and the photographer may sell individual photographs taken at the photo shoot to third parties for non-commercial/personal use but third parties are not granted any rights to publish or distribute any of the photographs without the agreement of the model and the photographer.

Definitions

"TFP" refers to "Time For Print" but here refers to digital copies of photographs instead of physical prints.

"Commercial Use" refers to any use of photographs by a business including advertisements/signs.
